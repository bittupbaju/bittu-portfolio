About

I started behind a camera — as a cameraman on live broadcasts, then as a cinematographer on documentaries, devotional productions, and a short film shot as director of photography. Along the way I picked up editing, colour grading, and the instinct for what makes a frame worth stopping on.

That instinct is what carried over when I moved into creative direction. The tools changed — from a viewfinder to a brand's full visual language — but the job is the same: notice what's worth showing, and show it well.
